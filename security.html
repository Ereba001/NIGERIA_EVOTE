Sovereign-Grade Security

Learn how the Nigeria E-Vote platform uses advanced cryptographic protocols to ensure 100% transparent and tamper-proof elections.

Immutable Blockchain Ledger

Every vote cast on our platform is cryptographically signed and submitted as a transaction to a private, distributed blockchain ledger. This ensures that once a vote is recorded, it cannot be altered, deleted, or tampered with by any entity, including election officials or system administrators.

  • Zero-Knowledge Proofs guarantee your vote remains anonymous.
  • Distributed nodes across Nigeria validate integrity continuously.

Multi-Factor Identity Gateway

We eliminate double voting and identity fraud through a stringent verification process. By linking the platform directly to the National Identity Management Commission (NIMC) database, we cross-verify your PVC against your NIN.

Authentication requires SMS OTP, a personal Security PIN, and optional biometric face/fingerprint matching via the mobile app.

Military-Grade Cloud Infrastructure

The entire E-Vote infrastructure is hosted on highly available, DDoS-protected sovereign cloud servers. Data in transit is secured via 256-bit SSL encryption, and all databases are air-gapped and encrypted at rest.

Independently Audited & Certified

Our systems undergo continuous penetration testing and security audits by top global cybersecurity firms to ensure readiness against any threat vectors.
