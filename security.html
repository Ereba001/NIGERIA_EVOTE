Security You Can Trust

See how Orivis keeps every decision transparent, secure, and tamper free — start to finish.

Permanent Blockchain Record

Every decision is cryptographically signed and stored on a private distributed ledger. Once recorded, nobody — not even system admins — can alter or delete it. You get full transparency without sacrificing privacy.

  • Zero knowledge proofs keep your choice anonymous.
  • Distributed nodes across regions verify integrity around the clock.

Multi Factor Identity Check

We prevent duplicate entries and identity fraud by linking directly to trusted verification systems. Your identity is checked against your Participant ID before you can participate.

Authentication uses SMS codes, a personal Security PIN, and optional biometric matching through the mobile app.

Enterprise Cloud Infrastructure

Orivis runs on highly available, DDoS protected cloud servers. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest, with databases isolated and locked down.

Audited by Top Security Firms

Our systems are tested and audited by leading cybersecurity firms to stay ahead of threats.
